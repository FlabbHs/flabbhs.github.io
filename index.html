Requiem Paranoia

Engine: Unreal Engine 5 - Sep 2025 - Dec 2025

ABOUT THIS GAME

Requiem Paranoia is a single player story based horror narrative featuring interactive puzzles, world exploration, and knowledge progression.

FEATURES I WORKED ON

Cutscene:

Look-Based Cutscene System

Implemented a custom cutscene system that triggers based on player gaze. By tracking how long the player looks at key objects, the system smoothly adjusts camera rotation and field of view to guide attention, temporarily locking focus before returning control.

Flashlight:

Created a flashlight pickup and on and off system.

Padlock:

Interact with lock dials!

Unlock doors!

Notes:

Read notes left behind from a mysterious stranger!
